**Business Plan: Importing Philippine Dried Mangoes to the Netherlands**

**1. Introduction**The global demand for convenient, healthy snacks is
fueling a booming trade in dried tropical fruits 1. The Netherlands,
serving as a primary logistics and trade hub for Europe, presents a
highly lucrative market for these products. This business plan outlines
a strategic approach to importing premium dried mangoes from the
Philippines into the Netherlands. By combining the Philippines\'
renowned agricultural quality with strict adherence to European Union
(EU) food safety regulations and a localized e-commerce presence, this
venture aims to capture a significant share of the European healthy
snack market 1, 2.

**2. The Proposal**We propose an import and distribution business
focused on bringing high-quality Philippine dried mangoes to Dutch
consumers and European wholesale buyers.

-   **Product Line:** Premium dried mangoes. We will align with 2025
    > consumer trends by offering \"clean label\" products (no added
    > sugar or sulfites), organic certifications, and innovative exotic
    > blends such as chili-seasoned mango 3.

-   **Sourcing:** We will partner exclusively with Philippine suppliers
    > who maintain international food safety standards (such as HACCP)
    > and provide full product traceability 2, 4.

-   **Technology & E-commerce:** We will utilize **Swell** as our
    > e-commerce platform to manage direct-to-consumer (DTC) and B2B
    > sales 5, 6. Swell is uniquely equipped for international
    > expansion, offering explicit multi-currency pricing across 230
    > currencies, content localization in 170 languages, and automated
    > regional tax compliance integrations 5, 7.

**3. Financial Overview**

-   **Sourcing & Quality Assurance Costs:** Initial capital will be
    > allocated to supplier verification, requiring mandatory
    > pre-shipment samples and third-party lab testing to ensure
    > products meet EU standards for moisture, sulfur dioxide, and
    > contaminants 2, 8.

-   **Logistics & Tariffs:** While sea freight is the most economical
    > option, air freight may be selectively utilized to prevent
    > humidity exposure and product caking 9. Financial planning must
    > account for import duty rates, which are tied to the specific
    > Harmonized System (HS) codes for dried fruits and can fluctuate
    > 10, 11.

-   **E-commerce Infrastructure:** Platform costs will be minimal and
    > scalable. Swell\'s Starter plan costs \$29/month (for up to \$50K
    > in annual sales), providing an enterprise-grade headless commerce
    > architecture without heavy initial IT investments 12.

**4. Roadmap**

-   **Phase 1: Supplier Verification & Philippine Compliance (Months
    > 1-2):** Identify Philippine exporters who are registered with the
    > Client Profile Registration System (CPRS) 13. Ensure they hold the
    > mandatory Food and Drug Administration (FDA) License to Operate
    > (LTO) and Certificate of Product Registration (CPR) 14, 15.

-   **Phase 2: EU Regulatory Alignment (Months 3-4):** Ensure all
    > products comply strictly with EU Food Law 16. This includes
    > meeting the Netherlands\' requirements for maximum allowable
    > pesticide residues and ensuring accurate labeling that clearly
    > states ingredients, allergens, country of origin, net weight, and
    > supplier details 2.

-   **Phase 3: Import Documentation & Shipping (Months 5-6):** Prepare
    > the critical import paperwork to prevent customs delays. This
    > includes the commercial invoice, packing list, bill of
    > lading/airway bill, import declaration, and a Phytosanitary
    > Certificate issued by the Philippine Bureau of Plant Industry
    > (BPI) proving the mangoes are pest-free 16, 17.

-   **Phase 4: Dutch Market Launch (Month 7):** Launch the localized
    > Swell e-commerce storefront in the Netherlands, optimized with
    > Dutch language content and Euro pricing 7.

**5. Execution Plan**

-   **Packaging & Storage:** To preserve the quality of the dried
    > mangoes during transit and warehousing, we will strictly enforce
    > the use of moisture-proof, food-grade films and desiccants (silica
    > gel packs) to prevent clumping 18. Pallets will be tightly
    > shrink-wrapped to prevent dust and tampering 18.

-   **Customs Clearance:** We will work closely with Dutch customs
    > officials to ensure the goods match the declared import paperwork,
    > comply with any import quotas, and that all corresponding duties
    > and taxes are paid accurately 10.

-   **Marketing & Subscription Sales:** We will capitalize on the
    > growing demand for subscription snack boxes 19. By leveraging
    > Swell\'s native subscription engine, we can offer flexible billing
    > intervals (monthly, quarterly, or custom schedules) and separate
    > invoicing from fulfillment, allowing Dutch consumers to receive
    > recurring deliveries of premium Philippine dried mangoes directly
    > to their doors 6.
