## What Is This?

**TropiFruit NL** is a planned import and distribution business bringing premium Philippine dried tropical fruits (starting with dried mangoes) to the Dutch market and beyond.

- 🇵🇭 **Source:** Direct from certified Philippine exporters
- 🇳🇱 **Target Market:** Netherlands as EU logistics gateway
- 🛒 **Channels:** Direct-to-Consumer (DTC) webshop + B2B wholesale
- 🔄 **Model:** Subscription-based recurring revenue + one-time orders
- 🌿 **Positioning:** Clean label, no added sugar, EU-compliant, traceable
